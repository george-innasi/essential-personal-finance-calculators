Essential Personal Finance Calculators

A growing hub of simple, honest calculators that answer the everyday money questions customers actually ask — clearly, and with the real math.

Why these exist. I've spent the past five years in the financial industry. These are the tools I always wished existed — to help customers understand the repeated questions I came across and the numbers I had to work out for them again and again. Each one turns a question I answered countless times at the counter into something anyone can see and explore for themselves.
